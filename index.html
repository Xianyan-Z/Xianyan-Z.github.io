About

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Metal Forming group in the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU). I focuse on developing novel metal forming techniques to produce complex parts with high performance and low cost aiming to solve critical industry problems in automotive, aerospace, etc. I has gained rich knowledge in solid mechanics, computational mechanics and physic-based plasticity and damage model development. Currently, she is engaged in developing an innovative mechanical calibration method to correct the dimensional deviation in thin-walled components such as the aluminium battery trays in electric cars.
